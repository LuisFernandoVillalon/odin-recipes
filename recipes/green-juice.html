Green Juice

A delicious, up-lifting, extravagant detoxing juice with tons of fibers and vegetables to get you right after a night of binge drinking or eating!

This green juice will help cleanse your colon and help your digestion get started. A beverage packed with antioxidants and all sorts of fruits and greens that your body will be thankful for it. It is a guaranteed that it will get you feeling better by getting you to the restroom and reliving your body from all unwanted bodily toxins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 green apples, halved🍏
  • 4 stalks of celery, leaves removed🥬
  • 1 cucumber🥒
  • 6 leaves of kale🥗
  • 1/2 lemon, peeled🍋
  • 1 (1 inch) piece of fresh ginger 👩‍🦰

Steps on How to Make:

  1. Insert the ingredients listed above through a juicer/blender.
  2. Drink your mouthwatering beverage.🤤🍹
