Green Juice

A up-lifting, detoxing green juice to get you right after a night of binge drinking or eating. :D

This green juice will help clean your colon and get your indigestion going. A beverage packed with antioxidants and fiber that will help your body fix itself at a faster pace. It is a guaranteed that it will get you feeling better by getting you to the restroom and reliving your body from unwanted bodily toxins.

Ingredients

  • 2 green apples, halved
  • 4 stalks of celery, leaves removed
  • 1 cucumber
  • 6 leaves of kale
  • 1/2 lemon, peeled
  • 1 (1 inch) piece of fresh ginger

Steps on How to Make

  1. Process ingredients listed above through a juicer.
  2. Drink your mouthwatering beverage. :D
